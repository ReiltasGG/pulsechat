NOVA TECH SOLUTIONS — COMPANY HANDBOOK
Last Updated: January 2024

=== COMPANY OVERVIEW ===

Nova Tech Solutions is a B2B SaaS company founded in 2018, headquartered in Austin, Texas. We build workflow automation tools for mid-size businesses across the United States. Our flagship product is FlowDesk, a no-code automation platform used by over 3,000 companies.

Our mission is to eliminate repetitive work for businesses so their teams can focus on what matters most.

=== WORKING HOURS & REMOTE POLICY ===

Nova Tech operates on a fully remote-first model. Core hours are 10am–3pm Central Time, Monday through Friday. Outside of core hours, employees are expected to respond to messages within 4 hours during their working day.

Employees may work from anywhere within the United States. International remote work is permitted for up to 30 days per year with manager approval.

=== TIME OFF POLICY ===

Nova Tech offers unlimited PTO. Employees are encouraged to take a minimum of 15 days off per year. Time off requests should be submitted in Rippling at least 5 business days in advance for periods of 3 days or more.

Public holidays observed: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (Thursday and Friday), Christmas Day.

Company-wide shutdown: Nova Tech closes for one week between Christmas and New Year's every year. This does not count against PTO.

=== COMPENSATION & PAYROLL ===

Employees are paid bi-weekly on Fridays via direct deposit. Payroll is processed through Rippling. Pay stubs are accessible in the Rippling dashboard.

Annual performance reviews occur in December. Merit increases take effect January 1st of the following year. The standard merit increase range is 3–7% based on performance rating.

Equity: All full-time employees receive stock options at hire. Vesting schedule is 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

=== BENEFITS ===

Health Insurance: Nova Tech covers 100% of employee premiums for medical, dental, and vision through Blue Cross Blue Shield. Dependents are covered at 80%.

401(k): Nova Tech offers a 401(k) through Fidelity with a 4% company match. Employees are eligible after 90 days of employment.

Home Office Stipend: Employees receive a one-time $1,000 home office setup stipend upon hire, and $500 annually for equipment and supplies.

Learning & Development: Each employee receives a $1,500 annual budget for courses, conferences, books, or certifications.

Mental Health: Nova Tech provides 12 free therapy sessions per year through Spring Health.

=== ONBOARDING ===

New employees begin with a 2-week onboarding program. Week 1 focuses on company culture, tools, and meeting your team. Week 2 is role-specific training.

Tools you will be set up with on day 1:
- Slack (internal communication)
- Notion (documentation and wikis)
- Linear (project management)
- GitHub (code repository)
- Figma (design — engineering and product teams)
- Rippling (HR and payroll)
- Google Workspace (email, calendar, docs)

Your manager will schedule a 30-60-90 day check-in to review your progress and goals.

=== PERFORMANCE REVIEWS ===

Nova Tech uses a bi-annual review cycle. Mid-year check-ins occur in June and formal reviews in December. Reviews are conducted in Lattice.

Performance ratings:
- Exceeds Expectations
- Meets Expectations
- Needs Improvement

Employees rated Needs Improvement for two consecutive cycles may be placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

=== CODE OF CONDUCT ===

Nova Tech is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive workplace. Discrimination or harassment of any kind is not tolerated and should be reported immediately to HR at hr@novatechsolutions.com.

Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to your manager and HR. This includes outside employment, investments in competitors, or personal relationships with vendors.

=== IT & SECURITY ===

All company devices must have full-disk encryption enabled. Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from public networks.

Passwords must be managed through 1Password. Sharing passwords via Slack, email, or any other channel is strictly prohibited.

Any suspected security incident must be reported to security@novatechsolutions.com immediately.

=== TRAVEL & EXPENSES ===

Business travel must be approved by your manager in advance. Book travel through TripActions. Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours. Business class requires VP approval.

Expense reimbursements are submitted in Rippling and processed within 2 pay cycles. Receipts are required for all expenses over $25.

Daily meal per diem during travel: $75.

=== OFFBOARDING ===

Employees resigning must provide a minimum of 2 weeks notice. Final paychecks are issued on the next regular pay date following the last day of work.

All company equipment must be returned within 5 business days of the last day. A prepaid shipping label will be provided by IT.

Access to all company systems is revoked on the last day of employment.
