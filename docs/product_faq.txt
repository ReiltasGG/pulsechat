NOVA TECH SOLUTIONS — FLOWDESK PRODUCT FAQ
Last Updated: January 2024

=== WHAT IS FLOWDESK? ===

FlowDesk is Nova Tech's flagship no-code workflow automation platform. It allows businesses to automate repetitive tasks across their existing tools without writing any code. FlowDesk connects to over 200 apps including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Gmail, QuickBooks, Shopify, and more.

=== PRICING ===

FlowDesk offers three pricing tiers:

Starter — $49/month
- Up to 5 users
- 1,000 automation runs per month
- 50 app integrations
- Email support

Growth — $149/month
- Up to 25 users
- 10,000 automation runs per month
- All 200+ integrations
- Priority email and chat support
- Custom branding

Enterprise — Custom pricing
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited automation runs
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- SSO and advanced security
- On-premise deployment option

All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

=== INTEGRATIONS ===

FlowDesk connects with over 200 applications. Most popular integrations include:

CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive
Email: Gmail, Outlook, Mailchimp
Communication: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoom
E-commerce: Shopify, WooCommerce, Stripe
Accounting: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks
Project Management: Asana, Trello, Linear, Jira
Storage: Google Drive, Dropbox, Box

Custom integrations are available on the Enterprise plan via our REST API and webhook support.

=== SECURITY & COMPLIANCE ===

FlowDesk is SOC 2 Type II certified. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are GDPR and CCPA compliant.

Data is hosted on AWS in US-East and EU-West regions. Enterprise customers can request dedicated infrastructure.
